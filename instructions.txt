RULES: Enter a number from 1 to 9 in each of the cells 
       in the grid ensuring each of the rows, columns,
       and square boxes contain each of these number 
       with no repeats

CONTROLS: 1) Mouse click or UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT arrow
             key to move 
            
          2) DELETE key to clear board

          3) BACKSPACE key to remove an entry

          4) Music keys: -F to forward
                         -B to back 
                         -P to pause/play

Press start to begin a new game or press enter to check
